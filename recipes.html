Sweet Potato Pie Recipe

Ingredient Amount Notes
Sweet Potatoes 1 & 1/2 cups Cooked, mashed
Butter 1/2 cup softened
Eggs 2 Well beaten
Cinnamon 1 TSP
Nutmeg 1 TSP
Allspice 1/8 TSP
Pumpkin Pie Seasoning 1/2 TSP
Vanilla Extract 1 TSP
Pie Shell 1 9-inch, Uncooked
Evaporated Milk 1 cup

Directions

  1. Prepare a big pot of boiling water. Add sweet potatoes to boiling water and boil for 20-25 minutes.
  2. While potatoes are boiling, preheat oven to 350 degrees and get your mixing bowl and spices ready.
  3. When potatoes are finished, run under cold water to aid in peeling off the skin.
  4. Mash the potatoes in the mixing bowl til they are real mashed.
  5. Add softened butter to the potatoes and mix it in until it melts.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix the sugar and spice ingredients.
  7. Add sugar and spices to the sweet potato mixture, as well as the vanilla
  8. Beat the eggs and add to the mixture
  9. Add evaporated milk little by little to the mixture, until consistency is like pancake batter
  10. Pour mixture in to pie shell
  11. Bake for 40 minutes in oven
  12. Enjoy!
